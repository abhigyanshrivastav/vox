Recording Guide for Speaker 01
--------------------------------------------
Instructions:
- Record in a quiet room.
- Keep the mic 10–20 cm away.
- Speak naturally, do not whisper.
- Record each sentence clearly.

Sentences to Record:
1. Secure channels must remain uncompromised at all times.
2. Authentication codes will rotate every five minutes.
3. Drone surveillance detected movement near the perimeter.
4. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
5. Mission authorization requires biometric verification.
6. Radio silence must be maintained during infiltration.
7. Encryption keys must never be transmitted verbally.
8. All personnel must verify identity using voice prints.
